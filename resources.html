Resources

This is a collection of some awesome assets, software and other bits and pieces that I've mentioned on the course, or that I use a lot. Mostly free and cross-platform where possible.

Visual assets

KENNEY.NL - Kenney's amazing artwork is freely available, and is what Gamedev 101 uses for it's examples. The artwork is free to use for anything you like, but donations are always welcome.

OPENGAMEART - Does exactly what it says on the tin. Community submitted artwork for games.

Useful software

SUBLIME TEXT - The only text editor you'll ever need.

AUDACITY - A deceptively excellent audio recording/editing application, perfect for working with lots of little samples. Exports nicely for Stencyl work too.

HEXELS - Awesome software that lets your draw in lots of weird and interesting ways, great for isometric work. Paid, but free version is great too.

GIMP - Free and cross-platform Photoshop clone. Very handy in a pinch.

BLENDER3D - Insanely powerful 3D package, free and open source.

Environments

UNITY - Massively popular and easy to use engine for games, allows you to make 2D and 3D games, lots of tutorials and documentation available, and a great community if you get stuck!
